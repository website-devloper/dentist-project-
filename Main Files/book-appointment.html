Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card (if applicable), and a list of any medications you're currently taking.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact us to verify your coverage.
